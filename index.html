Welcome to Conda

SIMPLE & CLEAN SHOPPING RESPONSIVE PRESTASHOP 1.7 THEME






Conda is a Prestashop theme ideal for Shopping Store.
The theme is built with latest web technologies and SEO optimized to make it friendly with search engines.

KEY FEATURES



FULLY RESPONSIVE

Integrating responsive function that gives your online store a beautiful interface on any high resolution devices or mobile and tables.

MEGAMENU

Our built-in mega menu is the perfect choice for large menus. You can set up columns and rows, use icons and images easily.

NEW TECHNOLOGY

We included the quickstart package in this theme. Just simple with a few clicks you can import easily CMS blocks and pages with sample content from the demo.

SAMPLE DATA

We included the quickstart package in this theme. Just simple with a few clicks you can import easily CMS blocks and pages with sample content from the demo.

AJAX CART

You can select product to buy without leaving current page, instantly add product to basket. Increase user experience and sales.

GOOGLE RICH SNIPPETS

Allows you to control appearance of your product such as product name, images, reviews and other useful information in organic Google search results

QUICK VIEW

Your product detail will open with full necessary information like: image product, product price, description, product reviews, product attributes.

Card title

This is a wider card with supporting text below as a natural lead-in to additional content. This content is a little bit longer.

EASY CUSTOMIZABLE

Easy change theme options such as homepage layout, color, menu styles, etc in admin panel without touching any code lines.
